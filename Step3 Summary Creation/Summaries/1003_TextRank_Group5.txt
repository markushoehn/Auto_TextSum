====================== General Summary of 1003 ======================
Teachers will meet and discuss the best alternative for that child, Please note, An IEP or 504 does not mean a parent is off the hook for their child's behavior.
A great person to share your thoughts and feelings with is your school counselor.
Officers have an option of giving kids a warning, taking them home, or booking them if the child is suspended or has been expelled, and the parents can't be reached.
