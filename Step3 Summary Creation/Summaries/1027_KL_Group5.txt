====================== General Summary of 1027 ======================
Enough is Enough is dedicated to protecting children and families from the Internet dangers of pornography and sexual predators so that they can enjoy the enormous benefits that the Internet offers.
Parents are often reluctant to discuss internet safety with their children simply because they aren't sure what to say.
Kids can build their own imaginary worlds and animals, play games, and interact with friends.
Children and teens are especially vulnerable and sensitive to what their peers think about them.
