====================== General Summary of 1032 ======================
Other types of social anxiety treatment are medication, which are usually recommended with the cognitive-behavior therapy.
Cognitive behavioral therapy, also known as CBT, has been empirically proven to ease the root causes of social anxiety disorder.
A child with an anxiety problem refuses to be comforted regardless of the circumstances.
Many times children will improve with the help of anti-anxiety or anti-depressive medication, such as Prozac, Paxil, or Zoloft.
