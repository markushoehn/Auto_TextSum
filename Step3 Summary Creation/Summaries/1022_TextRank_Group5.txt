====================== General Summary of 1022 ======================
Imbalance of Power : people who bully use their power to control or harm and the people being bullied may have a hard time defending themselves Dealing with childhood bullies can be a tough issue, whether your child is being bullied or is doing the bullying.
Children and adolescents who bully thrive on controlling or dominating others and have often been the victims of physical abuse or bullying themselves.
