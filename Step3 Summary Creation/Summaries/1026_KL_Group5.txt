====================== General Summary of 1026 ======================
Educate and Teach Your Children of the Dangers of Inhalants Abuse The aim is to teach children to be safe, to be aware of predatory strangers, and to be self protective.
Sexual predators no longer have to take to the streets to find their next possible victim.
Parents must know of and use the many resources available to help protect their children from the dangers of the internet.
Children of all ages are exposed to near ubiquitous access to the internet.
