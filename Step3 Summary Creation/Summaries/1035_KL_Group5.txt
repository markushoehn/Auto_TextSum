====================== General Summary of 1035 ======================
Kids often understand better than adults why they need to respect their bodies.
Parents and their kids need to sit down and talk about how they feel.
Children who learn how to relax their minds and bodies can invoke what they have learned in times of stress to alleviate feelings of tension and anxiety.
Kids and teens who are depressed are more likely to use alcohol and drugs than those who aren't depressed.
