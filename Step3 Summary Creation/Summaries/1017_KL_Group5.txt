====================== General Summary of 1017 ======================
Subsidized Stafford loans are awarded based on financial need, as the government pays the interest on these loans while the student is in school.
Unsecured student loans are the loans issued without any collateral or upfront payments.
Subsidized Student Loans are obtainable by students who demonstrate financial need.
Subsidized student loans are unique among all other student loans in that the federal government pays the interest on the loans while the student is in school.
