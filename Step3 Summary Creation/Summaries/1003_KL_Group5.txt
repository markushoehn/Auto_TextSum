====================== General Summary of 1003 ======================
If a parent SENDS the child the parent should not be held accountable for the child skipping out.
Teachers will meet and discuss the best alternative for that child, Please note, An IEP or 504 does not mean a parent is off the hook for their child's behavior.
Radical solutions can sometimes do more harm than good.
Parents, sisters and brothers, other family members, and teachers can help when someone is being left out or treated in a mean way.
