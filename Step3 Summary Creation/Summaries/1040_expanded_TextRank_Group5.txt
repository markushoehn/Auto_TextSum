====================== General Summary of 1040 ======================
Parents of children with learning disabilities need to recognize that children with learning disabilities require exceptional amounts of energy.
Many parents are prone to have feelings of guilt and their attitude is relayed to other members of the family.
ADHD is an omnipresent disorder, and the student will need to work with his or her physician to establish a treatment regime that reduces symptoms of ADHD and optimizes functioning, while minimizing any side effects.
Your child is not defined by his or her learning disability.
Encourage your learning disabled child to get outside, move, and play.
