====================== General Summary of 1001 ======================
ADHD is often difficult to treat, and most symptoms of ADHD respond well to stimulant medications.
A complementary treatment is added to usual treatment with the hope of even better control of ADHD symptoms.
After a child is diagnosed with attention deficit disorder (ADD ADHD), one of the most difficult decisions for a parent to make is whether to start him or her on medication .
Some people think diet supplements improve symptoms of ADHD.
If untreated, the disorder can have long-term adverse effects into adolescence and adulthood.
