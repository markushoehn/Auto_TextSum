====================== General Summary of 1023 ======================
Assure your child, if he or she did not provoke the bullying behaviour, that the problem lies with the bully and not with your child.
"Bullying is a real concern to families and parents of bullied children often find the situation traumatic and difficult to manage.
Adult intervention and guidance is essential in helping the victimized student and in reducing or eliminating the power imbalance between the parties involved in the bullying incident (Pepler & Craig, 2000; Shannon & McCall, n.d.).
