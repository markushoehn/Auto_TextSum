====================== General Summary of 1043 ======================
Children can receive one-on-one reading instruction with a trained tutor.
Whether your child has mild or severe Autism Spectrum Disorder, making reading a fun activity can help your child's learning and social skills.
Children can register with a parent or gaurdian or with a school class with their teacher as director.
Parents are important in helping their children get ready to read because: Children of all ages can benefit from being read to, and this can help instill a love of reading in your child.
