====================== General Summary of 1030 ======================
(6)  Corporal punishment shall not be administered on a student whose parent or guardian has stated in writing that corporal punishment shall not be administered to that student.Such corporal punishment shall be administered only in the presence of another member of the professional staff of the school.In no case shall the school administrator, teacher or individual engaged in child care exercising special parental authority inflict corporal punishment upon the child.Corporal punishment is not just a school issue involving students, but is a societal issue involving families and cultures.
