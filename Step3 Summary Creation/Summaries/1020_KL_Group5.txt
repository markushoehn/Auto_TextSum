====================== General Summary of 1020 ======================
Teenagers &amp; Drugs Drugs and Teenagers Drug use is the increasing problem among teenagers in today's high schools.
If problems are not addressed, teenagers can develop serious drug/alcohol problems, eating disorders, or suffer from depression and other emotional problems.
Teenagers at risk for developing serious alcohol and drug problems include those: Teens may use alcohol or drugs in an attempt to "self-medicate" their depression.
Remember that your role in Guided Self-Correction is to talk out the problem with your teenager, not to solve the problem.
