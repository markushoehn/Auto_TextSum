====================== General Summary of 1010 ======================
"The Court has opined that the only role of parents is to help their children in the education they receive in the institutional schools."
The parents are responsible for the child getting the compulsory education and the advancements are supervised by the home municipality.
DoDEA recognizes that home schooling is a sponsor's right and can be a legitimate alternative form of education for the sponsor's dependents.... Homeschooling is not technically recognized by the law in the NETHERLANDS .
Any parent can take advantage of his or her right to direct the child's education.
