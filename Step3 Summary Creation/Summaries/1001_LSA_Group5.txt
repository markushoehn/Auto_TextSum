====================== General Summary of 1001 ======================
Some kids simply cannot tolerate any stimulant, no matter how low the dosage.
Food can play a role in exacerbating, or keeping under control, symptoms of ADHD.
Talk to your health care practitioner (see Getting ADHD Help ) before trying any of these natural remedies.
Research has also shown that the simple elimination of sugar or candy does not affect ADHD symptoms, despite a few encouraging reports.
