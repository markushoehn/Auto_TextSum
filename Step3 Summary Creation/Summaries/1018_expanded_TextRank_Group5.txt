====================== General Summary of 1018 ======================
Federal Student Loans Federal student loans are essentially student loans for bad credit , as they do not require a credit check.
Private Student Loans and Cosigners Federal student loans do not require a cosigner, whereas, private student loans require a cosigner or a co-borrower.
Students with a bad credit history can apply for various loans and financial aid options including: Federal loans are perhaps the best and most affordable example of student loans without a cosigner; they do not require applicants to have a good credit history or a cosigner.
