====================== General Summary of 1039 ======================
(5) Learning of personal involvement is self-initiated, is pervasive, is evaluated by the learner and has meaning as its essence.
An open plan or variable space school or classroom is designed so that several classes of children and several teachers use a group of spaces in a variety of ways.
The student can act as a teacher and guide their parents through the process of leaving comments removing this possible barrier.
The teachers meet the parents regularly in their homes and at school, and inform them of the progress of the children.
