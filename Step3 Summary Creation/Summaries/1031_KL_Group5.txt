====================== General Summary of 1031 ======================
Parents can provide helpful home atmospheres that encourage their child to do their best.
Parents have to make sure their kids are committed to their online home school programs, which can be a daunting task considering that they do not have fixed standards of discipline to follow.
Student interaction with teachers is expected to be of a respectful nature, but is not overly formal.
Develop an effective discipline plan that integrates components of Discipline With Dignity.
