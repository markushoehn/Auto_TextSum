====================== General Summary of 1039 ======================
Referral concerns include most of the major diagnostic categories seen in adolescence (e.g.,schizophrenia and other psychotic disorders, mood disorders, anxiety and obsessive-compulsive disorders, disruptive behavior disorders) and challenges related to adolescence (e.g., risk taking behaviors, identity issues, substance use, relationship issues).
An open plan or variable space school or classroom is designed so that several classes of children and several teachers use a group of spaces in a variety of ways.
