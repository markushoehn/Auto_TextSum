====================== General Summary of 1046 ======================
Parents can learn from their children, and adults and children can learn at the same time.
Teachers can benefit from the insights of the parent and parents can benefit from the insights of the teacher.
Barriers to learning can take many forms including poverty, hunger, family breakdown and violence, child neglect and abuse, and sexual or substance abuse all of which threaten children's ability to learn and to develop into caring, competent and contributing adults.
