====================== General Summary of 1050 ======================
Teachers can benefit from the insights of the parent and parents can benefit from the insights of the teacher.
Parents blame teachers for not encouraging their child's intellect with challenging work.
The parents are certain that their child is gifted and that is the cause of the problems.
Teachers of the gifted can offer discussions of the unique sets of problems that face the gifted (Colangelo and Peterson, 1993).
