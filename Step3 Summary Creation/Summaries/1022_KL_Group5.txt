====================== General Summary of 1022 ======================
Many kids feel ashamed, or even believe what bullies tell them.
Children and adolescents who bully thrive on controlling or dominating others and have often been the victims of physical abuse or bullying themselves.
It turns out kids with severe food allergies are targets of bullying because of the food they can and can't eat.
Parents might notice kids acting differently or seeming anxious, or not eating, sleeping well, or doing the things they usually enjoy.
