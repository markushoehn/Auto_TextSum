====================== General Summary of 1042 ======================
Religious education in the integrated schools is provided partially through a common curriculum which seeks to emphasise shared Christian values.
Only those faiths considered to be the 'main religions in the school community' will be provided with State-funded faith-formation teachers, and those that do not qualify will be taught a common ethics programme." Strict church/state separationists also caution that with government money must come, over time, government supervision, and the application of non-discrimination statutes in matters of employment and retention, personal behavior or sexual orientation, religious beliefs, etc.
